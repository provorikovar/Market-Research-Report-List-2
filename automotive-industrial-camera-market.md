<p><strong>The Automotive Industrial Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Area Scan Cameras</li><li>Line Scan Cameras</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Industrial Camera market comprises three main types: Area Scan Cameras, Line Scan Cameras, and Others. Area Scan Cameras capture a complete image of the object, making them suitable for applications like object detection and quality control. Line Scan Cameras capture a single line of pixels at a time, enabling them to capture images of continuously moving objects, such as production lines. The Others category may include specialty cameras for specific automotive applications like infrared cameras for night vision or 3D cameras for advanced driver-assistance systems.</p></p>
