<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Industrial ball mills are used for grinding and blending materials in various industries. They come in two types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical ball mills are characterized by their vertical orientation and the grinding media is ground up against the inner walls of the mill. These mills are suitable for both wet and dry grinding. On the other hand, horizontal ball mills have a horizontal cylinder and the grinding media is rotated to grind the material. Horizontal ball mills are commonly used for fine grinding and require less space compared to vertical mills.</p></p>
