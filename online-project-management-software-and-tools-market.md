<p><p>Online project management software and tools are categorized into two main types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and maintained on the organization's local servers, allowing for more control over data security but requiring substantial upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Cloud-based software, on the other hand, is hosted on remote servers and accessed via the internet. It offers greater flexibility, scalability, and accessibility, eliminating the need for extensive hardware investments, while data management and security are taken care of by the service provider.</p></p>
