It has different generations: Gen1, Gen2, and Gen3. Gen1 provides a data transfer rate of 2.5 gigatransfers per second (GT/s), Gen2 doubles it to 5 GT/s, and Gen3 further doubles it to 8 GT/s. These market types represent the speed and capabilities of PCIe interfaces, with each generation offering faster data transfer rates, enabling better performance and supporting higher bandwidth requirements for modern applications and devices.</p></p>
