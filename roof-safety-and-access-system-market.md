<p><p>Roof safety and access system market offers a range of products to ensure worker safety while working at heights. Guardrails provide a protective barrier along the edges to prevent falls. Fall arrest equipment includes harnesses, lanyards, and lifelines to catch workers in case of a fall. Skylight screens are placed over fragile roof openings to prevent accidental falls. Roof anchors serve as secure attachment points for workers. Portable safety railings are flexible barriers that can be easily moved to different locations. Horizontal lifelines provide continuous fall protection along a horizontal path. Hatch systems are used for safe access through roof hatches.</p></p>
