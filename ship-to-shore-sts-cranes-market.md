<p><p>The Ship-to-Shore (STS) Cranes market can be categorized into three types: diesel, electric, and hybrid. Diesel cranes run on diesel fuel, providing high power and efficiency, but they emit harmful emissions. Electric cranes are powered by electricity, offering an eco-friendly solution with zero emissions. Hybrid cranes combine both diesel and electric technologies, allowing for flexibility in power source depending on operational needs and environmental regulations. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, which are considered by port operators when choosing the most suitable crane for their requirements.</p></p>
