<p><p>A Buckling Pin Relief Valve (BPRV) is a type of pressure relief valve that is designed to protect equipment against overpressure. It works by utilizing a buckling pin mechanism, which is a shear pin that is designed to buckle at a certain pressure, allowing the valve to open and relieve excess pressure. BPRVs are commonly used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and petrochemical.</p><p>The future outlook of the Buckling Pin Relief Valve Market is promising, with a projected compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.5% during the forecasted period.
