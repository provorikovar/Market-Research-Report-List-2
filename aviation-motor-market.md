<p><ul><li>DC Motor</li><li>AC Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation motor market consists of two main types: DC and AC motors. DC motors rely on direct current to operate, offering simplicity and control in applications such as landing gear, flap control, and wheel brakes. On the other hand, AC motors utilize alternating current and are known for their higher efficiency, better power factors, and ability to handle larger loads. AC motors find applications in aircraft systems like flight controls, fuel pumps, and air conditioning. Both DC and AC motors play critical roles in aviation, ensuring the functionality and safety of various aircraft components.</p></p>
