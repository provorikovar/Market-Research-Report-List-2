<p><p>The laboratory sieves market consists of three primary types: electromagnetic type, ultrasonic type, and others. The electromagnetic sieves utilize electromagnetic energy to generate vibrations, allowing for efficient and accurate particle separation. Ultrasonic sieves utilize ultrasonic waves to generate high-frequency vibrations, ensuring precise particle analysis. The "others" category encompasses additional sieve types such as air jet sieves and sonic sieves. These sieves provide different methods of particle separation for laboratory testing and analysis, catering to various research needs and requirements.</p></p>
