<p><p>The powdered goat milk market offers two main types: whole milk and skim milk. Whole milk powder is made by evaporating and drying goat milk, preserving its full fat content. This type of milk powder is rich and creamy, providing a high level of nutrition and flavor. On the other hand, skim milk powder is produced by removing the fat content from goat milk. Skim milk powder is lower in fat and calories but still contains essential nutrients, making it a healthier option for those seeking a lower-fat alternative.</p></p>
