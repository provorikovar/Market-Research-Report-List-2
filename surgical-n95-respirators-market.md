<p><p>Surgical N95 respirators are designed to provide both surgical and respiratory protection to medical professionals. There are two main types: flat-fold and cup style. Flat-fold respirators are lightweight, disposable masks that can be easily folded and stored when not in use. They provide a close facial fit, ensuring maximum filtration efficiency. On the other hand, cup style respirators have a more rigid shape and provide a tighter seal around the face. This design enhances comfort and prevents leakage, making them suitable for longer wear. Both types are essential in healthcare settings to protect against airborne particles and droplets.</p></p>
