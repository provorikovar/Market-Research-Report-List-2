<p><p>The digital audio processor market can be classified into two main types: single channel and multi-channel digital audio processors. A single channel digital audio processor is designed to process and enhance the audio signal of a single channel, such as a microphone or instrument. On the other hand, a multi-channel digital audio processor is capable of processing and enhancing multiple audio channels simultaneously. These processors are commonly used in audio mixing consoles, home theater systems, and professional audio setups to improve the quality and intelligibility of sound.</p></p>
