<p><p>The orthopedic felt market offers two types of products: those with adhesive back and those without adhesive back. Orthopedic felt with adhesive back contains a layer of adhesive that allows for easy application and attachment to various surfaces. Both types serve the same purpose of providing cushioning and support in orthopedic applications, but their difference lies in the presence or absence of adhesive backing.</p></p>
