<p><ul><li>Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP)</li><li>Bi-Level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP)</li><li>Automatic Airway Pressure (APAP)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sleep apnea machines market consists of three main types: Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP), Bi-Level Positive Airway Pressure (BiPAP), and Automatic Airway Pressure (APAP) machines. CPAP machines deliver a continuous and consistent air pressure to keep the airways open during sleep. BiPAP machines provide two different pressure levels, higher during inhalation and lower during exhalation, making it more comfortable for some users. APAP machines adjust the air pressure automatically throughout the night, based on the individual's breathing patterns. These machines are commonly used to treat sleep apnea and improve quality of sleep.</p></p>
