<p><p>The road bike headlight market can be classified into three types: Halogen, LED, and Others. Halogen headlights have a traditional incandescent bulb that emits a warm, yellowish light, providing decent visibility at night. LED headlights utilize light-emitting diodes that emit a brighter, whiter light, offering enhanced visibility and better energy efficiency. Other types may include HID (High-Intensity Discharge) or laser headlights, which provide even brighter illumination. Each type has its own advantages and drawbacks, allowing cyclists to choose the best option based on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
