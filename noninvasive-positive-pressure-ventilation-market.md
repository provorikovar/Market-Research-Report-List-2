<p><p>Noninvasive Positive-pressure Ventilation (NPPV) Market Types include Continuous Positive Airway Pressure Ventilator (CPAP), Automatic Positive Airway Pressure Ventilator (autoPAP), Dual Horizontal Positive Airway Pressure Ventilator (BiPAP), and Others. CPAP delivers a constant pressure to keep the airways open, primarily used for sleep apnea treatment. AutoPAP adjusts the pressure based on the patient's needs throughout the night. BiPAP delivers two different pressures for inhalation and exhalation, suitable for patients with respiratory muscle weakness. Other NPPV devices may include advanced features like inspiratory and expiratory assistance. These ventilators provide noninvasive respiratory support and are used in various respiratory conditions.</p></p>
