<p><p>The market for sponge applicators can be divided into two main types: latex sponge and non-latex sponge. However, concerns about latex allergies have spurred the development of non-latex sponges made from synthetic materials like polyurethane foam. Non-latex sponges offer similar performance but are hypoallergenic, making them a preferred choice for individuals with latex sensitivities. Both types cater to different customer needs and preferences within the sponge applicator market.</p></p>
