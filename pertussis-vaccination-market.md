<p><strong>The Pertussis Vaccination Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acellular Vaccines</li><li>Whole-cell Vaccines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pertussis vaccination market is categorized into two main types: acellular vaccines and whole-cell vaccines. Acellular vaccines are made by purifying specific components of the bacteria responsible for pertussis, while whole-cell vaccines involve using inactivated or killed whole pertussis bacteria. Acellular vaccines are preferred due to their reduced side effects and effectiveness in preventing pertussis. However, whole-cell vaccines are still used in some countries where they are considered more affordable or offer broader protection. Both types aim to provide immunity against pertussis, commonly known as whooping cough.</p></p>
