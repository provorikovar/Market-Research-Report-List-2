These indicators typically have a dial face that shows data such as speed, temperature, or pressure on a scale. </p><p>In contrast, Digital Display Type ship indicators utilize electronic screens to present data, usually in numerical or graphical format. These indicators display information such as position, speed, or fuel levels digitally, making them more precise and easier to read.</p></p>
