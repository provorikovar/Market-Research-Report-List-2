<p><p>The high-speed surgical drill market consists of two main types: pneumatic and electric. Pneumatic high-speed surgical drills are powered by compressed air and are known for their lightweight and compact design. They offer high torque and speed control, making them ideal for delicate procedures. On the other hand, electric high-speed surgical drills use electric motors for power. They offer higher precision and speed, along with adjustable settings. Both types cater to the needs of surgeons and play a crucial role in various surgical procedures.</p></p>
