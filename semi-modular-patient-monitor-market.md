<p><p>The semi-modular patient monitor market includes two types: wired network and wireless network. In the wired network market, patient monitors are connected to the central monitoring station through wired connections, such as Ethernet cables. This allows for real-time transmission of patient data, providing healthcare professionals with immediate access to vital information. On the other hand, the wireless network market utilizes wireless technology, such as Bluetooth or Wi-Fi, to establish connections between patient monitors and the central monitoring station. This enables the flexibility of mobility and eliminates the need for physical wires, enhancing ease of use and convenience.</p></p>
