<p><p>Investment management tools can be categorized into two main types: on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises tools are installed and run on the user's own infrastructure, providing more control and customization but requiring maintenance and hardware investments. On the other hand, cloud-based tools are accessible online and hosted on remote servers, offering flexibility, scalability, and automatic software updates but relying on internet connectivity and third-party data security measures. Both types aim to assist professionals in managing investments effectively, but they differ in deployment and operational aspects.</p></p>
