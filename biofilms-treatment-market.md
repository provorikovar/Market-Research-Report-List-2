<p><p>Biofilms treatment refers to the management and removal of biofilms, which are complex communities of microorganisms that adhere to surfaces and develop a protective matrix. Several factors are driving this growth, including the increasing prevalence of biofilm-associated infections, the rise in microbial resistance to conventional antimicrobial agents, and the growing awareness about the impact of biofilms on healthcare and industrial sectors. Furthermore, the advancements in biofilm detection techniques and the development of novel treatment strategies are also contributing to market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing research and development activities in the field of biofilm treatment are expected to further propel market growth.
