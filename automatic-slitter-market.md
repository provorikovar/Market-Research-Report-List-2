<p><p>The automatic slitter market consists of two main types: hydraulic slitter and CNC slitter. Hydraulic slitters utilize hydraulic power to operate the slitting process. They offer a high level of precision and efficiency, making them ideal for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, CNC slitters use computer numerical control technology for precise and automated cutting. They offer advanced features like programmable settings and high-speed cutting, making them suitable for complex and high-volume cutting tasks. Both types cater to various industrial needs, providing efficient and accurate slitting solutions.</p></p>
