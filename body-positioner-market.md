<p><p>The body positioner market encompasses two main types: whole body positioners and half body positioners. Whole body positioners are designed to support and align the entire body, providing comfort and minimizing pressure on specific areas. They are commonly used in healthcare settings to assist patients with limited mobility or recovering from surgery. On the other hand, half body positioners focus on particular body parts, such as the legs or upper body, and are often used in medical and therapeutic settings to promote proper alignment and relieve pressure on specific areas of the body, such as the spine or joints.</p></p>
