<p><p>Manifold valves are used to control the flow of liquids or gases in various industries. The market for manifold valves includes different types such as 2 valve manifolds, 3 valve manifolds, and 5 valve manifolds. </p><p>2 valve manifolds are commonly used for isolation and calibration purposes, allowing users to isolate one instrument from another. </p><p>3 valve manifolds are designed for differential pressure applications, enabling users to measure and control pressure between two points. </p><p>5 valve manifolds are typically utilized for double block and bleed functions, providing a higher level of safety by isolating and venting multiple instruments simultaneously.</p></p>
