<p><ul><li>Low Temperature Knudsen Cells</li><li>Medium Temperature Knudsen Cells</li><li>High Temperature Knudsen Cells</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knudsen Cells are widely used in material science research and chemical analysis. Low Temperature Knudsen Cells are designed to operate at lower temperatures (below 400°C), making them suitable for studies related to low melting point materials. Medium Temperature Knudsen Cells operate at higher temperatures (400-800°C) and are commonly used in vapor pressure measurements. High Temperature Knudsen Cells are capable of operating at even higher temperatures (above 800°C), making them ideal for experiments involving high melting point materials or thermal treatment of samples. Each type of Knudsen Cell offers specific temperature ranges to cater to different research needs.</p></p>
