<p><p>The three phase power transformer market is classified into three voltage segments: low-voltage, medium-voltage, and high-voltage. Low-voltage transformers handle electricity with a voltage rating between 208 to 480 volts and are commonly used in residential and small commercial applications. Medium-voltage transformers operate within the range of 2,401 to 69,000 volts and are primarily utilized for commercial or industrial purposes. High-voltage transformers deal with voltages above 69,000 volts and are mainly employed in power transmission and distribution networks for long-distance electricity supply.</p></p>
