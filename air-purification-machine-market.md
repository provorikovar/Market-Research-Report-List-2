<p><p>The air purification machine market consists of three main types: passive, active, and active and passive hybrid. Passive air purifiers rely on natural processes like gravity to trap pollutants and require no external power source. Active air purifiers use technologies such as ionization or ozone generation to actively remove pollutants from the air. Active and passive hybrid purifiers combine both passive and active technologies to efficiently eliminate contaminants. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, depending on the specific needs and preferences of the consumer.</p></p>
