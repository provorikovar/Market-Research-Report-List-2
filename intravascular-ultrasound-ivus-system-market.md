<p><p>The Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) System market includes three types: IVUS Consoles, IVUS Catheters, and IVUS Accessories. IVUS Consoles are the main control units used to operate the IVUS system and analyze the ultrasound images. IVUS Catheters are thin tubes with an integrated ultrasound probe that is inserted into blood vessels to capture high-resolution images of the arterial walls. IVUS Accessories include guidewires, sheaths, and balloons, which assist in the proper placement and movement of the catheters during the procedure. These components collectively enable healthcare professionals to examine and diagnose cardiovascular conditions accurately.</p></p>
