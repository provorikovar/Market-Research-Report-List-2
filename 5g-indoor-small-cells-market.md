<p><p>The 5G Indoor Small Cells market consists of two main types, namely Sub-6GHz and mmWAVE. Sub-6GHz refers to frequencies below 6 gigahertz and offers wide coverage area and moderate data speeds. It is ideal for providing reliable indoor connectivity in large buildings or dense urban areas. On the other hand, mmWAVE operates at millimeter wave frequencies, offering extremely high data speeds but limited coverage area. It is more suitable for small indoor spaces or specific high-density areas where ultra-fast data transfer is required.</p></p>
