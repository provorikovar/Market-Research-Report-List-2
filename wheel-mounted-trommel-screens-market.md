<p><p>Wheel mounted trommel screens are machines used to separate materials by size. They are typically used in industries such as mining, construction, and recycling to sort and separate various types of materials such as soil, compost, gravel, and debris.</p><p>These trommel screens consist of a cylindrical drum that is inclined at an angle and mounted on a wheeled chassis. As the drum rotates, the materials are fed into the drum, and the finer particles pass through the screen while the larger particles are discharged from the end of the drum. Wheel-mounted trommel screens are highly portable and can be easily transported to different sites for various applications.</p><p>The wheel mounted trommel screens market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. One of the key drivers for this growth is the increasing demand for efficient and cost-effective screening solutions in industries such as mining and construction.
