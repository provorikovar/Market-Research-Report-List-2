<p><p>The Medical Imaging Diagnostic Equipment Market encompasses various types of imaging technologies used in diagnosing medical conditions. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) uses strong magnetic fields and radio waves to generate detailed images of the internal body parts. Computed Tomography (CT) scans utilize X-rays to create cross-sectional images of bodily structures. Ultrasonography (USG) involves the use of high-frequency sound waves to produce images of organs and tissues. Nuclear Medicine Imaging (NMI) employs radioactive substances to examine organ function and diagnose diseases. These imaging technologies play a crucial role in clinical decision-making and enhancing patient care.</p></p>
