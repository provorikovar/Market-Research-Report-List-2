<p><p>The Aseptic Isolators market is divided into two types: Open Isolators and Closed Isolators. Open Isolators are designed with a physical barrier that separates the operator from the environment, but still allows for the exchange of air and materials. They are suitable for applications that require frequent transfers of materials. Closed Isolators, on the other hand, provide a completely sealed environment, preventing any exchange of air or materials. They are ideal for highly sensitive operations that require a high level of sterility and protection.</p></p>
