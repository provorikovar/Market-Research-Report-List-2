<p><p>The inorganic ceramic ultrafiltration membrane market consists of two main types: tubular membrane and flat-sheet membrane. Tubular membranes are cylindrical in shape and typically have a higher packing density, allowing for a higher filtration capacity. On the other hand, flat-sheet membranes have a larger surface area, enabling a higher flux rate. Both types are used in various applications such as water treatment, food and beverage processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemical processing.</p></p>
