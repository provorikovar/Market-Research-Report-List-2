These instruments include tambourines, recorders, ocarinas, melodions, xylophones, rhythm sets/handbells, harmonicas, and others. Tambourines are handheld percussion instruments with metal jingles, whereas recorders are wind instruments that resemble flutes. Ocarinas are ancient clay wind instruments, while melodions are keyboard-like instruments. Xylophones are percussion instruments with wooden bars of varying lengths. Rhythm sets or handbells include a collection of different percussive instruments, while harmonicas are small wind instruments. The market also encompasses other instruments suited for classroom use.</p></p>
