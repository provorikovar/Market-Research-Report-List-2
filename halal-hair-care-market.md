This can be attributed to the increasing awareness among consumers about the benefits of halal products and the growing Muslim population worldwide. These ingredients are known for their nourishing and moisturizing properties, which appeal to consumers looking for effective and safe hair care solutions. Furthermore, there is a rising demand for halal hair care products that are free from harmful chemicals, such as sulfates, parabens, and alcohol.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing the launch of specialized halal hair care products catering to specific hair concerns like dandruff, hair loss, and damaged hair.
