<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Overcurrent Protection Relay market includes various types of relays used to safeguard electrical systems from overcurrent faults. Instantaneous Overcurrent Relays provide immediate tripping upon detection of excessive currents, while Definite Time Overcurrent Relays offer a fixed time delay before tripping. Inverse Time Overcurrent Relays vary the time delay based on the magnitude of the current, while Directional Overcurrent Relays trip only when the fault current flows in a specific direction. These relays play a crucial role in protecting electrical equipment and preventing damage in case of overcurrent conditions.</p></p>
