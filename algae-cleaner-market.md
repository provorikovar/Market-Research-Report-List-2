<p><strong>The Algae Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oxidation Type</li><li>Non-Oxidation Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Algae Cleaner market can be divided into two main types: Oxidation Type and Non-Oxidation Type. </p><p>The Oxidation Type algae cleaners use chemicals or substances that have oxidizing properties to eliminate algae from various surfaces. These cleaners work by breaking down the algae's cellular structure, making it easier to remove. </p><p>On the other hand, Non-Oxidation Type algae cleaners do not rely on chemical oxidation. Instead, they utilize natural biological agents or physical methods to control algae growth. These cleaners often include enzymes or microorganisms that can inhibit algae development without harming the environment.</p></p>
