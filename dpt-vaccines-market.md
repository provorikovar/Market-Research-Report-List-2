<p><p>DPT vaccines are a type of combination vaccine that provides immunization against three diseases: diphtheria, pertussis (whooping cough), and tetanus. There are different types of DPT vaccines available in the market. DTaP is a vaccine given to infants and young children, which contains an acellular form of the pertussis vaccine. Tdap is a booster vaccine given to adolescents and adults, which combines the acellular pertussis component with tetanus and diphtheria. DTwP is an older version of the vaccine that uses whole-cell pertussis and is primarily used in countries where acellular vaccines are not available or affordable.</p></p>
