<p><p>Rotary Variable Differential Transformers (RVDT) are devices used to measure angular displacement. They comprise of a primary coil that generates an alternating current (AC) or a direct current (DC) signal, and two secondary coils whose outputs indicate the angular position of a rotating object. AC RVDTs are commonly used in applications where accuracy and precision are crucial, while DC RVDTs are preferred in systems requiring faster response times. Both types find applications in industries such as aerospace, defense, and industrial manufacturing.</p></p>
