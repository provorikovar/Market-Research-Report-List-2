<p><p>High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) is a technique used to separate, identify, and quantify components in a mixture. HPLC devices consist of a stationary phase, a mobile phase, and a detector. The stationary phase is a solid or liquid, which retains the sample components, while the mobile phase carries the sample through the column. The detector analyzes the separated components and provides data for further analysis.</p><p>The High Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) Device Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period.
