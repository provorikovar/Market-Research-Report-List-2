<p><p>The manually caulking gun market can be categorized into two types: closed barrel caulking guns and open barrel caulking guns. Closed barrel caulking guns feature a tube-like structure where the caulk is stored, and require puncturing of the cartridge to dispense caulk. On the other hand, open barrel caulking guns have a removable barrel that allows for easy loading and changing of cartridges, eliminating the need for puncturing. Both types are widely used in construction, woodworking, and DIY projects for precise application of caulk and sealants.</p></p>
