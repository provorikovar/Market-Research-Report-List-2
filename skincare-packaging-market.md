<p><p>Skincare packaging market is divided into three main types: plastic, glass, and other. Plastic packaging dominates the market due to its affordability, flexibility, and durability. Glass packaging, although relatively expensive, is preferred by brands aiming for a premium, luxurious image. It offers an excellent barrier against moisture and oxygen, ensuring product integrity. "Other" refers to alternative materials like metal or paperboard, which are used to create unique and sustainable packaging solutions for eco-conscious brands.</p></p>
