<p><p>The market for Automatic Burst Strength Testers can be categorized into two types: Pneumatic Bursting Strength Testers and Hydraulic Bursting Strength Testers. Pneumatic Bursting Strength Testers utilize compressed air to apply pressure to a sample and measure its bursting strength. On the other hand, Hydraulic Bursting Strength Testers use hydraulic fluid to generate pressure for testing the burst strength of a material. Both types of testers are used in various industries to determine the strength and durability of materials, such as textiles, paper, and packaging.</p></p>
