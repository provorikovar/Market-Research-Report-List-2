The market types include solids, sludges, leachate, ammonia, BOD, and COD materials. Solids refer to solid waste, while sludges are semi-solid materials. Leachate refers to liquid waste that seeps through the ground. Ammonia is a type of chemical waste, while BOD (biochemical oxygen demand) and COD (chemical oxygen demand) represent organic and inorganic waste substances. The deep-well disposal services market caters to the safe and efficient disposal of these different types of waste materials using underground wells.</p></p>
