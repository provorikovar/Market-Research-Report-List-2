<p><strong>The Thermoelectric Cooling Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Stage Modules</li><li>Multi-stage Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermoelectric cooling devices are electronic systems that use the Peltier effect to cool or heat an object. Single-stage modules, also known as single-stage thermoelectric coolers (TECs), are commonly used in small cooling applications and have a single layer of Peltier junctions. On the other hand, multi-stage modules, also known as multi-stage TECs, have multiple layers of Peltier junctions, allowing for more precise temperature control and higher cooling capacity. These modules find applications in larger cooling systems, such as refrigeration units or advanced temperature control devices.</p></p>
