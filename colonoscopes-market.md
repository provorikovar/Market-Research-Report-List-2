<p><p>Colonoscopes are medical devices used for diagnostic and interventional procedures in the colon. Conventional colonoscopes are flexible tubes with a light and camera, allowing direct visualization of the colon lining. They are commonly used for screening and detecting abnormalities. In contrast, virtual colonoscopy utilizes CT scans to create 3D images of the colon, eliminating the need for invasive procedures. This type is less invasive and is used for patients unable to undergo conventional colonoscopies. Both types contribute to the colonoscopy market, providing options for different patient needs.</p></p>
