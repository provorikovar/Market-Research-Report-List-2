<p><p>The air cushion machine market comprises various types of air cushions used for packaging purposes. Air pillows are flexible, inflatable cushions made of strong materials. Grid air cushions have a grid-like pattern and offer better protection for fragile items. Bubble packaging consists of air-filled bubbles between two layers of plastic film. Packing air cushions are used to fill empty spaces in packages. Other types of air cushions include wedges, sleeves, and corner protectors. These cushioning options provide efficient and reliable protection for products during shipping and handling.</p></p>
