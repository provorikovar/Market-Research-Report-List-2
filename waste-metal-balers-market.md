<p><ul><li>Vertical Waste Metal Balers</li><li>Horizontal Waste Metal Balers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vertical Waste Metal Balers are machines used to compress and bale waste metals vertically. They are suitable for industries that produce a large amount of bulky metal waste, such as car manufacturing or metal fabrication. These balers have a vertical design, allowing them to occupy less floor space and have a smaller footprint. Horizontal Waste Metal Balers, on the other hand, compress waste metals horizontally. They are commonly used in smaller industries or facilities with limited floor space. These balers have a horizontal design and can be easily integrated into existing waste management systems.</p></p>
