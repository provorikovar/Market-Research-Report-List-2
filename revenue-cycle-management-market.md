<p><p>Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) refers to the process of managing and tracking a patient's financial journey throughout their healthcare experience. The RCM market can be categorized into two types: On-premise and Cloud-based solutions. On-premise RCM involves the installation and management of software directly on the organization's servers. It offers complete control and customization but requires higher maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based RCM utilizes internet-based platforms to deliver services remotely.
