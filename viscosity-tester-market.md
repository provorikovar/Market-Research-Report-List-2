<p><p>Viscosity testers are used to measure the viscosity of fluids, which is a measure of its resistance to flow. There are different types of viscosity testers available in the market. Engler Viscosity test measures the time taken for a fixed volume of fluid to flow through a specific orifice. Sagbolt Viscosity test measures the rate of flow of a fluid through a small orifice under specific temperature and pressure conditions. Redwood Viscosity test measures the time taken for a specific volume of fluid to flow through a calibrated orifice.</p></p>
