<p><p>The portable multimeter market consists of two main types: digital multimeters and analog multimeters. Digital multimeters use digital displays to present measurement values, making them easier to read and providing accurate results. They typically include additional features like auto-ranging and data logging capabilities. On the other hand, analog multimeters use a scale and a moving needle to indicate readings, providing a more traditional and simple approach. While analog multimeters are less precise and lack advanced features, they are still preferred by some professionals for their simplicity and durability.</p></p>
