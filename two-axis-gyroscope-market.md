<p><p>The Two-Axis Gyroscope market is segmented into various types. MEMS Gyroscope, one of the market segments, utilizes motion sensors and microelectronics to measure rotation. Another segment is the Ring Laser Gyroscope (RLG), which employs laser beams to detect rotation. Fiber-Optic Gyroscope (FOG) uses optical fibers to measure rotation by examining the interference pattern of light waves. Hemispherical Resonator Gyroscope (HRG) operates based on mass displacement to detect rotation. Dynamically Tuned Gyroscope (DTG) measures rotation using a vibrating mechanical structure. Lastly, there are other types of gyroscopes available in the market with different mechanisms to measure rotation.</p></p>
