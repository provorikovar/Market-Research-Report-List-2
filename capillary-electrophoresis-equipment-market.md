<p><p>Capillary electrophoresis equipment is a laboratory technique used to separate and analyze molecules based on their size and charge. It involves the use of a capillary tube filled with an electrolyte solution, through which an electric current is applied. As the sample passes through the capillary, the molecules are separated based on their charge-to-size ratio, allowing for the identification and quantification of different components.</p><p>The global capillary electrophoresis equipment market is experiencing significant growth and is projected to expand at a CAGR of 9.2% during the forecast period. Several factors are contributing to this growth. Capillary electrophoresis offers high resolution, sensitivity, and automation, making it a preferred choice for molecule separation and analysis.</p><p>Additionally, the increasing emphasis on drug discovery and development, along with the growing need for personalized medicine, is fueling the adoption of capillary electrophoresis equipment.
