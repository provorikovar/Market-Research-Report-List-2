<p><p>The Telecom Power Supply market consists of two main types: DC Power and AC Power. DC Power refers to direct current, which is a constant flow of electrical charge in one direction. It is commonly used in telecom infrastructures due to its efficiency and ability to support long-distance transmission. AC Power, on the other hand, stands for alternating current, where the flow of charge periodically reverses direction. It is typically used in residential and commercial settings due to its easy distribution and ability to power different devices simultaneously.</p></p>
