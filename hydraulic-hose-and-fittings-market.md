<p><p>The hydraulic hose and fittings market can be categorized into two main types: wire braided and spiral wire hydraulic hose fittings. Wire braided hydraulic hose fittings consist of a strong inner tube made of synthetic rubber, reinforced by multiple layers of braided steel wire. These fittings are flexible and suitable for medium pressure applications. On the other hand, spiral wire hydraulic hose fittings have a high tensile steel wire braid that provides maximum strength and durability, making them ideal for high-pressure applications. Both types play a crucial role in efficiently transmitting hydraulic power in various industries.</p></p>
