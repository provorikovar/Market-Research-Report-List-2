<p><p>The papermaking machine market includes two main types: fully-automatic devices and semi-automatic devices. Fully-automatic machines offer advanced technology and are capable of performing all stages of papermaking without human intervention. These machines are highly efficient and provide a smoother production process. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some level of human involvement, such as loading raw materials or controlling settings. While they may not offer the same level of automation as fully-automatic devices, semi-automatic machines are more affordable and suitable for smaller scale papermaking businesses.</p></p>
