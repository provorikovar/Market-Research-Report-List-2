Granular baits consist of small pellets that are scattered in areas where roaches frequent. These bait types are ingested by the pests and cause extermination. Gels baits come in tubes and are applied in cracks and crevices, acting as a food source for roaches. These products effectively control roach populations by targeting their feeding habits and eliminating them.</p></p>
