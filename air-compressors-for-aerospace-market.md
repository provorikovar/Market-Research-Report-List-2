<p><p>The aerospace industry utilizes two types of air compressors: air cooled type and water cooled type. Air cooled compressors are designed to cool the compressed air using ambient air and are simpler to install and maintain. On the other hand, water cooled compressors use water as a cooling medium, providing efficient cooling and reducing the risk of overheating, but require a water source and additional plumbing for operation. Both types offer their own advantages and are chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the aerospace industry.</p></p>
