<p><p>The submarine fiber optic cable market can be categorized into two types: single core and multicore. Single core cables consist of a single optical fiber, which can transmit data at high speeds over long distances. On the other hand, multicore cables contain multiple optical fibers, allowing for greater capacity and bandwidth. These cables are used for undersea communication networks, enabling the transfer of data, voice, and video signals across continents. The choice between single core and multicore cables depends on the specific requirements of the network and the desired capacity.</p></p>
