<p><p>Vacuum quench furnaces are used in various industries for heat treating processes. There are two types of vacuum quench furnaces: vacuum oil quenching furnaces and vacuum gas quenching furnaces. Vacuum oil quenching furnaces use oil as a quenching medium to cool down the treated material, while vacuum gas quenching furnaces use gases such as nitrogen or helium. Both types offer controlled cooling rates, improved hardness, and minimized distortion of the material being treated, resulting in enhanced mechanical properties and high-performance end products.</p></p>
