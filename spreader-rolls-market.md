<p><ul><li>Fixed Spreader Rolls</li><li>Adjustable Spreader Rolls</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spreader Rolls market consists of two main types: Fixed Spreader Rolls and Adjustable Spreader Rolls. Fixed Spreader Rolls are designed to maintain a fixed gap between two rolls, ensuring uniform tension and thickness of materials being processed. On the other hand, Adjustable Spreader Rolls allow for the customization of the roll gap, allowing operators to modify tension and thickness according to their specific needs. Both types play a crucial role in various industries such as paper production, printing, and film processing by ensuring consistent results and reducing defects.</p></p>
