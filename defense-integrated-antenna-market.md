<p><p>The Defense Integrated Antenna market comprises various types of antennas used in defense applications. Aperture antennas use a large aperture to receive or transmit signals. Array antennas consist of multiple antenna elements arranged in a specific pattern, enabling beamforming and improved performance. Microstrip antennas are compact and lightweight, utilizing printed circuit board technology. Wire antennas are simple and cost-effective, typically used for short-range communications. Each antenna type caters to different defense requirements, such as long-range surveillance, compact size for unmanned systems, or low-cost solutions for tactical communications.</p></p>
