<p><p>The small distribution transformers market comprises pole-mounted and pad-mounted transformers. Pole-mounted transformers are installed on utility poles and are commonly used in urban areas. They are compact and designed to withstand harsh weather conditions. On the other hand, pad-mounted transformers are installed on concrete pads at ground level. They are often preferred in suburban and rural areas due to their low-maintenance requirements and aesthetic appeal.
