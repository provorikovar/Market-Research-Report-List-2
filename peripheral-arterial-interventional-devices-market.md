<p><p>The peripheral arterial interventional devices market includes balloon dilatation catheters and stents. Balloon dilatation catheters are medical devices used to open narrowed or blocked blood vessels, allowing improved blood flow. They are inserted into the artery and inflated, expanding the vessel and restoring blood flow. Stents are small mesh-like tubes placed in the artery to keep it open and prevent it from narrowing again. These devices are essential in treating conditions like peripheral artery disease and are widely used in interventional cardiology procedures.</p></p>
