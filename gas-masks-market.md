<p><p>Gas masks are protective respiratory devices used to filter out harmful airborne particles and contaminants. The market for gas masks can be categorized into various types. Disposable respirators are single-use masks that provide basic protection against particles. Air purifying respirators (APRs) use cartridges or filters to remove contaminants from the air. Powered air purifying respirators (PAPRs) use a fan to pull air through filters. Self-contained breathing apparatus (SCBAs) provide the highest level of respiratory protection, with a portable air supply. Lastly, there are other types of gas masks that offer unique features or cater to specific industries.</p></p>
