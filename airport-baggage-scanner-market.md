<p><p>The Airport Baggage Scanner market consists of two main types: X-ray scanners and Millimeter Wave scanners. X-ray scanners use high-energy electromagnetic radiation to create images of the contents of baggage, allowing security personnel to detect any potential threats. On the other hand, Millimeter Wave scanners use radio waves to generate a 3D image of the scanned objects, enabling a more detailed analysis. Both types of scanners play a crucial role in enhancing airport security by efficiently identifying any suspicious items within passengers' baggage.</p></p>
