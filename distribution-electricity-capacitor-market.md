<p><strong>The Distribution Electricity Capacitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shunt Capacitor</li><li>Series Capacitor</li><li>Circuit Breaker Capacitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The distribution electricity capacitor market consists of three main types: shunt capacitors, series capacitors, and circuit breaker capacitors. Shunt capacitors are connected in parallel with the power system to improve power factor and reactive power compensation. Series capacitors are connected in series with the transmission lines to enhance the power transfer capability. Circuit breaker capacitors are used in circuit breaker installations to improve the breaker's switching voltage capacity. These different capacitor types serve different purposes in the distribution electricity network to ensure efficient power transmission and distribution.</p></p>
