<p><strong>The Stand Alone Smoke Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionization Smoke Detector</li><li>Photoelectric Smoke Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stand-alone smoke detector market consists of two main types: Ionization Smoke Detectors and Photoelectric Smoke Detectors. Ionization smoke detectors utilize a small radioactive source to ionize the air, and when smoke enters the detector, it disrupts the current flow, triggering the alarm. On the other hand, photoelectric smoke detectors work by using a light source and a sensor. When smoke particles enter the detector, they scatter the light beam, causing the sensor to activate the alarm. Both types offer effective smoke detection capabilities, catering to different needs and preferences.</p></p>
