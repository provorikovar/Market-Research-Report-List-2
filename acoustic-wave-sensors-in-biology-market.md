<p><p>Acoustic wave sensors are widely used in the field of biology for various applications. Two popular types of acoustic wave sensors in this market are Quartz Crystal Microbalance (QCM) sensor and Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensor. The QCM sensor utilizes a quartz crystal to detect small changes in mass on its surface, providing accurate and sensitive measurements. On the other hand, the SAW sensor uses surface acoustic waves to detect changes in its surface properties, allowing for precise detection of biological molecules. These sensors play a crucial role in various biological studies and applications.</p></p>
