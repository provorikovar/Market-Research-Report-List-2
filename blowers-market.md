<p><p>Blowers are commonly used in industries to provide a continuous flow of air or gas. There are three main types of blowers on the market. First, centrifugal blowers use rotating impellers to increase the kinetic energy of the air or gas, pushing it outward. Second, positive-displacement blowers use a set of meshing lobes or gears to trap and move air or gas. Lastly, there are other types of blowers that include regenerative blowers, rotary lobe blowers, and screw blowers, each with distinct mechanisms to generate and handle airflow.</p></p>
