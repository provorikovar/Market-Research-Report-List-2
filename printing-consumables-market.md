<p><strong>The Printing Consumables Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet Consumables</li><li>Laser Consumables</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printing consumables market consists of two main types: inkjet consumables and laser consumables. Inkjet consumables are used in inkjet printers and include ink cartridges and print heads. These consumables use liquid ink and are known for producing high-quality color prints. On the other hand, laser consumables are used in laser printers and include toner cartridges and drums. Laser consumables use toner powder and are ideal for high-volume printing with crisp and professional black-and-white prints. Both types of consumables play a crucial role in ensuring consistent print quality and reliable performance in their respective printers.</p></p>
