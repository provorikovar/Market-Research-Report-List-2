<p><p>The 3D Printed Metals market includes two main types: Metal-Filled Rods and Filaments. Metal-Filled Rods are solid cylindrical structures made of a combination of metal particles and a binder material. These rods are used in a process called direct metal deposition, where the metal particles are melted and deposited to create a 3D object. On the other hand, Filaments are thin threads of metal that are fed into a 3D printer. They are melted and extruded layer by layer to create intricate metal objects.</p></p>
