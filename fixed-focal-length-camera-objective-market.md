<p><p>A fixed focal length camera objective, also known as a prime lens or a single focal length lens, is a type of camera lens with a fixed focal length. These lenses are highly appreciated for their ability to capture sharp images with minimal distortion and aberrations.</p><p>The global fixed focal length camera objective market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.7% during the forecast period.
