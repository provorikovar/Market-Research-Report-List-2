<p><p>A nephroureteral stent is a medical device that is used to treat conditions in the urinary system, specifically to relieve obstruction in the ureter and allow urine flow from the kidney to the bladder. <p><p>The Nephroureteral Stent Market comprises different types of stents used in medical procedures. The sealing bracket stent is designed with a bracket to prevent the stent from moving out of place. Closed support stents provide support to the kidney and ureter by maintaining proper positioning. The Double J tube stent has two J-shaped loops, which prevent migration and maintain drainage. Multi-loop J tube stents have multiple J-shaped loops for enhanced stability and drainage, particularly in complex cases. These stents play a crucial role in managing urinary tract obstructions and optimizing patient outcomes.</p></p>
