<p><p>Computer-Aided Design (CAD) market is divided into two main types: Electronic CAD (ECAD) and Mechanical CAD (MCAD). ECAD primarily focuses on the design and development of electronic circuits and systems. It enables engineers to create and simulate electronic designs, like printed circuit boards (PCBs) and integrated circuits (ICs). On the other hand, MCAD is dedicated to the design and modeling of mechanical systems and components. It supports engineers in creating 3D models, analyzing tolerances, and generating manufacturing specifications for mechanical products. Both ECAD and MCAD technologies significantly enhance productivity and accuracy in their respective domains.</p></p>
