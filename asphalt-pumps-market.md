<p><p>The Asphalt Pumps market includes various types of pumps used in the asphalt industry. Gear pumps are the most common type, based on toothed gears to move the asphalt. Screw pumps use intermeshing screws for asphalt transfer. Rotary vane pumps utilize rotating vanes in a cylindrical chamber to move the asphalt. Other types comprise centrifugal pumps, which use centrifugal force, and lobe pumps, which use two rotating lobes. Each pump type has its own advantages and applications in the asphalt industry, depending on factors such as viscosity and flow rate requirements.</p></p>
