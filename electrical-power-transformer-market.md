<p><p>The electrical power transformer market is categorized into three types based on voltage level: low voltage transformers, medium voltage transformers, and high voltage transformers. Low voltage transformers are typically used in residential and commercial applications, converting electric energy from mains voltage to a lower level for devices. Medium voltage transformers are used in industrial settings to transfer electricity within a distribution network. High voltage transformers are primarily employed in power transmission systems, converting electricity to very high voltages for efficient long-distance electrical distribution. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the required voltage level.</p></p>
