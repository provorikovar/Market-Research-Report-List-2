<p><strong>The GPS Antenna Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vertical Polarization</li><li>Circular Polarization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS Antenna Market consists of different types of antennas such as vertical polarization and circular polarization. Vertical polarization antennas radiate signals in a vertical orientation, providing improved signal reception for devices that rely on such orientation, such as GPS terrestrial stations. On the other hand, circular polarization antennas emit signals in both vertical and horizontal orientations simultaneously, allowing for better reception in challenging environments where signal interference or multipath reflections might occur, ensuring more accurate GPS positioning for devices such as navigational systems or drones.</p></p>
