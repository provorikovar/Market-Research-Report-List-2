<p><p>Polyurethane thermoset foam insulation material can be classified into two main types: rigid and flexible. </p><p>Rigid foam insulation is a high-density material that offers excellent thermal resistance. It is commonly used in applications such as wall insulation, roofing, and other areas that require structural support.</p><p>Flexible foam insulation, on the other hand, is a low-density material that provides good flexibility and ease of installation. It is often used in applications that require conformability and cushioning, such as packaging materials and HVAC systems.</p><p>These foam insulation materials cater to different needs and applications, providing effective insulation and energy efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
