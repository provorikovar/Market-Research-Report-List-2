<p><p>Product Lifecycle Management (PLM) is a process that includes the planning, creation, development, and disposal of a product. The PLM market types can be categorized as cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based PLM solutions are hosted on remote servers and accessed over the internet, offering scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness. On the other hand, on-premises PLM solutions are installed and operated locally, providing greater control, security, and customization options. Both market types cater to the diverse needs of businesses and organizations, with cloud-based being more suitable for those seeking agility and accessibility, and on-premises for those prioritizing control and security.</p></p>
