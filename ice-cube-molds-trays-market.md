<p><p>Ice cube molds and trays are kitchen utensils used to freeze water or other liquids into ice cubes of various shapes and sizes. The rising trend of cocktail culture and the popularity of fancy-shaped ice cubes for aesthetic purposes are also driving the market.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is benefiting from the technological advancements in ice cube molds and trays, such as the introduction of silicone molds that offer flexibility and ease of use.
