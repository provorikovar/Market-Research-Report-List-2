<p><p>A Dedicated Outdoor Air System (DOAS) is a type of HVAC system that is designed to provide ventilation and climate control for commercial buildings. It works by delivering fresh outside air to the building while simultaneously removing the stale indoor air. DOAS typically consists of separate air handling units for ventilation and cooling, ensuring that the supply air is clean, conditioned, and at the desired temperature and humidity levels.</p><p>The DOAS market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years due to various factors. Increasing awareness about indoor air quality and the importance of proper ventilation in commercial buildings has been a major driver for market growth. Additionally, stricter regulations and building codes regarding energy efficiency and sustainability have also contributed to the increased adoption of DOAS.</p><p>Moreover, the demand for DOAS has been driven by the growing trend of green building construction and LEED certification, which require efficient and sustainable HVAC systems. Furthermore, advancements in technology have led to the development of more advanced and energy-efficient DOAS systems, further fueling the market growth.</p><p>According to market research, the Dedicated Outdoor Air System (DOAS) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12% during the forecast period.
