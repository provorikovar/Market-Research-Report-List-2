<p><p>The Piezoelectric Buzzer market consists of two main types: Active Piezo Buzzer and Passive Piezo Buzzer. Active Piezo Buzzers generate sounds through an electronic circuit, requiring an external power supply for operation. They offer versatility in sound production and are preferred for applications where distinct tones are required. On the other hand, Passive Piezo Buzzers produce sounds without the need for an external power source by utilizing the piezoelectric effect. They are simpler in design but offer limited sound options. Both types find applications in various industries including automotive, consumer electronics, and telecommunications.</p></p>
