<p><p>The construction payroll tool market is categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based construction payroll tools operate on remote servers, allowing users to access and manage payroll information through an internet connection. This type offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness since there are no infrastructure or maintenance requirements. On the other hand, on-premises construction payroll tools are installed on a local server, requiring physical hardware and software management. While providing greater control and security, this type necessitates higher upfront costs and maintenance responsibilities for the users.</p></p>
