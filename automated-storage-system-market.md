<p><p>The automated storage system market consists of various types, including unit load, mini load, and others. Unit load automated storage systems are designed to handle large quantities of goods on pallets or containers, providing efficient storage and retrieval of these unit loads. On the other hand, mini load automated storage systems are suitable for smaller items, utilizing automated cranes or shuttles to manage high-density storage and faster picking. The "Others" category encompasses alternative storage systems such as carousel systems or vertical lift modules, which optimize space utilization and enhance order picking efficiency.</p></p>
