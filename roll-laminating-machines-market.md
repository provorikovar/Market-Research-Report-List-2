<p><p>Roll Laminating Machines are used to apply a protective film onto documents, photos, or other materials. The market for these machines is divided into two types: Cold Roll Laminating Machines and Hot Roll Laminating Machines. </p><p>Cold Roll Laminating Machines use pressure-sensitive films that adhere to the materials without the need for heat. They are often used for heat-sensitive documents or materials.</p><p>Hot Roll Laminating Machines use a combination of heat and pressure to melt the adhesive on the film, creating a strong bond with the materials. They are commonly used for laminating thicker or more durable materials.</p></p>
