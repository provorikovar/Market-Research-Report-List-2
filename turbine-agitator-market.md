<p><p>The turbine agitator market comprises various types of agitators, including top-entry, side-entry, bottom-entry, and other agitators. Top-entry agitators are inserted from the top of the tank and are commonly used for mixing applications in large tanks. Side-entry agitators are mounted on the side of the tank and are ideal for continuous mixing in various industries. Bottom-entry agitators are installed from the tank bottom and are suitable for blending heavy and solid materials. Other agitators include magnetic agitators and portable agitators, which offer unique mixing capabilities for specific applications.</p></p>
