<p><p>The wafer handling equipment market can be categorized into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic equipment is characterized by its ability to handle the entire wafer handling process without human intervention. It efficiently executes tasks such as loading and unloading wafers, aligning, and transferring them between different equipment. On the other hand, semi-automatic equipment still requires some degree of human involvement, such as manually placing or removing wafers, but it automates other functionalities like alignment and transfer. Both types cater to the needs of different industries and are used in various semiconductor manufacturing applications.</p></p>
