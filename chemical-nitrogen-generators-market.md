<p><p>Chemical nitrogen generators are used to produce nitrogen gas for various industrial applications. There are three main types of chemical nitrogen generators: pressure swing adsorption (PSA), membrane, and cryogenic air. </p><p>PSA generators use adsorbent materials to remove oxygen and other impurities from compressed air, producing high-purity nitrogen gas. </p><p>Membrane generators employ semipermeable membranes to separate nitrogen from other gases in the air, while cryogenic air generators cool air to extremely low temperatures, condensing oxygen and leaving behind pure nitrogen gas.</p></p>
