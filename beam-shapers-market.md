<p><p>Beam shapers are optical devices used to modify the shape of laser beams for specific applications. Top-Hat Beam Shapers create a flat-topped beam, which is ideal for laser machining or material processing, as it distributes energy evenly across the target. Flat-Top Beam Shapers produce a beam with a uniform intensity profile, suitable for scanning applications or precision measurements. Both types have their unique benefits and are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, medical, and scientific research to enhance the quality and precision of laser-based processes.</p></p>
