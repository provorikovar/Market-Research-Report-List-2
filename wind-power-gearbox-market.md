<p><p>The wind power gearbox market can be categorized into four types: planetary gears, spur gears, bevel gears, and helical gears. Planetary gears are compact and offer high torque density, while spur gears are simple and cost-effective. Bevel gears are used for changing the direction of power transmission, and helical gears provide smooth and quiet operation. These different types of gears are crucial in wind turbines to efficiently convert the rotational speed of the rotor into electrical energy.</p></p>
