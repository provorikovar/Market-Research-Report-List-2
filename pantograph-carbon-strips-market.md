<p><strong>The Pantograph Carbon Strips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Metal-impregnated Carbon Strip</li><li>Pure Graphite Carbon Strip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pantograph carbon strips are used in applications that require precise and smooth movement. Metal-impregnated carbon strips are made by infusing metal particles into the carbon structure, which enhances conductivity and strength. They are commonly used in high-current electrical contact applications. On the other hand, pure graphite carbon strips are made entirely of graphite, offering excellent self-lubricating properties and heat resistance. They are often used in applications where low friction and high temperature resistance are required, such as in mechanical seals or piston rings.</p></p>
