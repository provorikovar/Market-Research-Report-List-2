<p><p>Scanning pens can be categorized into two market types: off-line scanning pens and on-line scanning pens. Off-line scanning pens are portable devices that allow users to scan text offline and later transfer it to a digital format for translation or other purposes. On the other hand, on-line scanning pens require a constant internet connection to instantly translate scanned text. Both types of pens offer convenience and efficiency in capturing and translating written content, but off-line pens offer more flexibility while on-line pens provide real-time translation capabilities.</p></p>
