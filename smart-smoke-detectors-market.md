<p><p>The Smart Smoke Detectors market includes various types of detectors such as Photoelectric Smoke Detectors, Ionization Smoke Detectors, and Combination Smoke Detectors. Photoelectric Smoke Detectors use a light source and sensor to detect visible smoke particles. Ionization Smoke Detectors employ a radioactive element that ionizes the air and detects the resulting changes caused by smoke. Combination Smoke Detectors combine both photoelectric and ionization methods for more comprehensive smoke detection. Each type has its advantages and is designed to cater to different environments and types of fires.</p></p>
