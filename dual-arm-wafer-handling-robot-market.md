<p><ul><li>Atmospheric Robot</li><li>Vacuum Robot</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dual arm wafer handling robot market includes two types: atmospheric robot and vacuum robot. An atmospheric robot is designed to handle wafers in a cleanroom environment, using mechanical arms and various end-effectors. It is suitable for handling wafers during the transfer process. On the other hand, a vacuum robot utilizes vacuum suction cups to pick and place wafers efficiently. It is commonly used in applications where a gentle touch and precise handling is required to prevent any damage to the wafers. Both types play a significant role in the wafer handling process.</p></p>
