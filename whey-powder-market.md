<p><p>Whey powder is a popular dairy-based product used in various industries. The market for whey powder can be divided into two types: concentrate form and isolate form. Whey concentrate powder is produced by separating the whey protein from the liquid portion of milk. It contains slightly more lactose and fats but retains more bioactive compounds. On the other hand, whey isolate powder undergoes additional processing to remove lactose and fats, resulting in a purer form of protein. Both market types serve different purposes and cater to different consumer demands.</p></p>
