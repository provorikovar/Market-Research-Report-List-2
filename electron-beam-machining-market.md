<p><p>Electron Beam Machining (EBM) is a manufacturing process that utilizes high-speed electron beams to remove material from a workpiece. It is commonly used in three market types: welding, surface treatment, and drilling. In welding, EBM is used to join metal parts together by melting them under the electron beam. Surface treatment involves applying controlled heat to modify the surface properties of a material, such as hardening or tempering. Lastly, EBM drilling is employed to create precise holes in various materials using the concentrated energy of the electron beam.</p></p>
