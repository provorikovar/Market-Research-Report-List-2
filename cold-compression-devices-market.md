<p><p>Cold compression devices are medical devices used to provide a combination of cold therapy and compression therapy to treat musculoskeletal injuries and post-operative conditions. These devices, also known as cold therapy machines, typically consist of a cold pack or gel wrap and a compression wrap that is applied to the affected area of the body. They are used to reduce pain, swelling, inflammation, and muscle spasms by numbing the affected area and improving blood flow.</p><p>The global cold compression devices market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The market growth can be attributed to several factors such as an increasing number of sports injuries, rising prevalence of arthritis and other musculoskeletal disorders, and growing awareness about the benefits of cold compression therapy.
