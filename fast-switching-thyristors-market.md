<p><p>Fast switching thyristors are electronic devices that can switch on and off quickly, making them suitable for various applications. There are two market types for fast switching thyristors: standard and medium frequency. Standard fast switching thyristors are designed for general-purpose applications that demand high voltage and current capabilities. On the other hand, medium frequency fast switching thyristors are specifically developed for use in medium-frequency circuits, where high-power applications require efficient switching at frequencies ranging from a few kilohertz to several hundred kilohertz. These market types cater to different requirements of users in terms of voltage, current, and frequency capabilities.</p></p>
