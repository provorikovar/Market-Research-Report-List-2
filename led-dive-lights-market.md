<p><p>The LED Dive Lights market consists of two main types: stationary and portable. Stationary dive lights are typically fixed and mounted in a specific location, such as on a dive platform or underwater structure. These lights provide a continuous source of illumination for divers in a specific area. On the other hand, portable dive lights are designed to be handheld and carried by divers. These lights offer greater mobility and flexibility, allowing divers to explore different areas and navigate in dark underwater environments. Both types cater to the lighting needs of divers but differ in terms of installation and portability.</p></p>
