<p><p>The emerging inkjet printing market encompasses two main types: demand inkjet printing technology and continuous inkjet printing technology. Demand inkjet printing technology refers to the process of printing on demand, where images or documents are produced based on specific user requests. Continuous inkjet printing technology, on the other hand, involves the ongoing, non-stop printing of images or texts using a continuous stream of ink droplets. Both market types cater to the growing demand for efficient and high-quality printing solutions.</p></p>
