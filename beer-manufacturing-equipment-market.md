<p><p>The beer manufacturing equipment market can be categorized into various types. Mashing equipment is used for the initial step of brewing where grains are mixed with water and heated. Fermentation equipment facilitates the conversion of sugars into alcohol and carbon dioxide during the brewing process. Cooling equipment helps in maintaining the temperature during fermentation. Storage equipment includes tanks and barrels to store the brewed beer. Compressors are used for providing compressed air to various equipment in the brewery. 'Others' category includes additional equipment like filters, pumps, and piping systems, among others.</p></p>
