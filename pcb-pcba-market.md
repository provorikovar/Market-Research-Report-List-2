<p><p>PCBs (Printed Circuit Boards) are the foundation of electronic devices, connecting components through conductive pathways. In the PCB market, various types exist. Rigid 1-2Sided PCBs are the simplest, commonly used in basic applications. Standard Multilayer PCBs allow dense component placement. HDI (High-Density Interconnect) PCBs enable smaller components and higher circuit density. Microvia or Build-Up PCBs provide more complex interconnections. IC Substrates are specialized PCBs for integrated circuits. Flexible Circuits are bendable, suited for compact devices. Rigid Flex PCBs combine rigid and flexible substrates. Other types include specialty PCBs like ceramic, aluminum, or metal core boards.</p></p>
