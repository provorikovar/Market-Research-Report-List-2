<p><p>The electronics desiccants market is comprised of various types including clay desiccants, silica gel, calcium oxide, and others. Clay desiccants are natural materials with high water absorption capacity, commonly used to control humidity in electronic devices. Silica gel is a synthetic material that also has high moisture absorption properties and is available in different forms such as beads, gels, and powders. Calcium oxide, also known as quicklime, is another desiccant option that effectively absorbs moisture. Other desiccant options in the market include molecular sieves, activated carbon, and zeolites, each with unique properties for moisture control in electronics.</p></p>
