This approach can lead to improved treatment outcomes and reduced adverse effects.</p><p>Another trend is the emergence of targeted therapies. Several biologic drugs, such as anti-fibrotic agents and immunosuppressants, have shown promise in clinical trials for systemic sclerosis treatment. These targeted therapies aim to block specific pathways involved in fibrosis and immune dysregulation, providing more specific and effective treatment options.</p><p>Moreover, there is an increasing emphasis on a multidisciplinary approach to systemic sclerosis management.
