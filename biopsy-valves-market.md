<p><p>Biopsy valves are utilized in medical procedures for extracting tissue samples from patients. There are two main types of biopsy valves available in the market: disposable valves and reusable valves. Disposable valves are designed for single-use only, ensuring sterile and contamination-free procedures. They need to be discarded after each use. On the other hand, reusable valves are sterilizable and can be used multiple times, making them more cost-effective in the long run. The choice between these two types depends on factors such as the procedure's complexity, cost considerations, and hygiene requirements.</p></p>
