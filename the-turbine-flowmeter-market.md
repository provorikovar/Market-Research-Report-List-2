<p><p>The Turbine Flowmeter is a type of flowmeter that measures fluid flow by using a rotor mounted on bearings and suspended in the fluid stream. This velocity is then used to calculate the volumetric flow rate.</p><p>The Turbine Flowmeter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) of 5.3% during the forecast period.
