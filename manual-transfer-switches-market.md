<p><strong>The Manual Transfer Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Panel Mounted</li><li>Din Rail Mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Manual transfer switches are devices used to transfer power supply from the main source to an alternative source during power outages. Panel-mounted manual transfer switches are installed directly onto the electrical panel and allow for easy switching between power sources. On the other hand, din rail-mounted manual transfer switches are mounted on a din rail within the electrical panel, making it easier to install and remove. Both types serve the same purpose of ensuring uninterrupted power supply and are widely used in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
