<p><p>Door position switches are devices used to determine the open or closed position of a door. There are several types available in the market. Concealed SPDT magnetic switches are hidden within the door frame, providing a discreet and secure installation. Concealed/flush mount magnetic switches are also hidden but designed to be mounted flush with the door frame for a seamless appearance. Surface mount magnetic switches, on the other hand, are installed on the surface of the door frame. These switches come in various types, catering to different installation preferences and requirements.</p></p>
