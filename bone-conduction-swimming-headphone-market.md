<p><p>Bone conduction swimming headphones are available in two types - wired and wireless. The wired type consists of a headphone with a cable that needs to be physically connected to the audio device. These headphones provide a reliable and uninterrupted connection but limit movement due to the cable. On the other hand, wireless bone conduction swimming headphones are not connected by cables and use Bluetooth technology to establish a connection with the audio device. This type offers greater freedom of movement but might experience occasional interruptions or connectivity issues.</p></p>
