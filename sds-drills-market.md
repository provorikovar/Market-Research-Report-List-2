<p><strong>The SDS Drills Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cordless SDS Drill</li><li>Rope SDS Drill</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SDS drills are categorized into two main types, cordless SDS drills and rope SDS drills. Cordless SDS drills are powered by rechargeable batteries, providing the convenience of portability and eliminating the need for an electrical outlet. These drills are commonly used in construction, carpentry, and other DIY projects. On the other hand, rope SDS drills are operated using a rope mechanism, allowing for increased power and efficiency. They are primarily employed in heavy-duty applications, such as drilling into concrete or masonry. Both types cater to different user preferences and specific drilling requirements.</p></p>
