<p><p>Salon management software refers to the technology solutions used by salon owners and managers to streamline their operations. Cloud-based software is hosted and accessed through the internet, allowing for easy and remote management, data backup, and scalability. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed and operates on the salon's local servers or computers. This type offers greater control and data privacy, but may require more maintenance and upfront costs. Both types of software cater to the specific needs of salon businesses, offering various features such as appointment scheduling, inventory management, staff management, and customer relationship management.</p></p>
