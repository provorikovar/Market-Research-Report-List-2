<p><p>The UHF RFID Inlays market consists of two main types: UHF Dry Inlays and UHF Wet Inlays. UHF Dry Inlays are typically used for labeling, tracking, or identification purposes. They are made of a thin layer of RFID antenna attached to a substrate material and are suitable for applications where the RFID inlay will be directly attached to an item or surface. On the other hand, UHF Wet Inlays are designed for embedding into materials or objects, such as packaging, ID cards, or product labels, allowing for seamless integration without affecting the appearance or function of the item.</p></p>
