<p><p>The elevator interlock market is divided into different types based on the technology used. Firstly, electrical elevator interlocks utilize electrical systems to prevent the elevator doors from opening unless the cab is present at that floor. Secondly, mechanical elevator interlocks rely on mechanical systems to achieve the same purpose. Thirdly, mixed elevator interlocks combine both electrical and mechanical mechanisms for enhanced security. Lastly, "Others" category includes interlocks that incorporate alternative technologies not falling into the previous categories. These types of interlocks ensure safer elevator operations by preventing unauthorized access and accidents.</p></p>
