<p><p>Tracheobronchial stent grafts are medical devices used to treat airway obstruction in the trachea and bronchi. There are three main types of stent grafts available in the market. Self-expandable stents are made of a flexible material that expands on its own after insertion. Non-expandable stents, on the other hand, remain the same size after insertion. Balloon-expandable stents are inserted and expanded using a balloon catheter. Each type of stent graft offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the patient and the severity of their airway blockage.</p></p>
