<p><p>High temperature semiconductor devices are electronic components specifically designed to operate efficiently at high temperatures. The adoption of WBG materials is expected to accelerate in the coming years, further driving market growth.</p><p>Another trend in the market is the integration of advanced thermal management technologies in high temperature semiconductor devices.
