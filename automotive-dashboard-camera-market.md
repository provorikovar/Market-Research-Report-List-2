<p><strong>The Automotive Dashboard Camera Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Multi-Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the automotive dashboard camera market, there are two main types available: single channel and multi-channel cameras. </p><p>A single channel camera, as the name suggests, captures footage from only one direction. It is designed to monitor the front or rear of the vehicle, providing a clear view of the road or capturing incidents happening ahead or behind the car. </p><p>On the other hand, a multi-channel camera records video from multiple angles simultaneously. These cameras have multiple lenses and can record footage from the front, rear, and sides of the vehicle, providing a more comprehensive view of the surroundings.</p></p>
