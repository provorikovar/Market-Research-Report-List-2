<p><ul><li>Direct Composite Veneers</li><li>Indirect Composite Veneers</li><li>Prefabricated Veneers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite veneering systems refer to various methods used in dental procedures to enhance the appearance of teeth. The market is categorized into three main types: direct composite veneers, indirect composite veneers, and prefabricated veneers. Direct composite veneers involve applying composite resin directly onto the tooth surface. Indirect composite veneers involve fabricating thin shells outside the mouth and bonding them to the teeth. Prefabricated veneers are pre-made and require minimal modification before being bonded to the teeth. These different types offer dentists and patients flexibility in choosing the most suitable approach for their specific needs.</p></p>
