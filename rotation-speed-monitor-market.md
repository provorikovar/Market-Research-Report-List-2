<p><strong>The Rotation Speed Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inductance</li><li>Capacitance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotation speed monitor market offers two common types: inductance and capacitance. Inductance-based monitors detect rotational speed by measuring changes in the inductance of a coil attached to the rotating object. Capacitance-based monitors, on the other hand, determine rotational speed by analyzing changes in the capacitance of a capacitor placed near the rotating object. Both types provide reliable and accurate monitoring of rotation speed in various industrial applications, ensuring smooth operations and optimum performance.</p></p>
