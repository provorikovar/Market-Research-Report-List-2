<p><p>Visual project management solutions include cloud-based and on-premise market types. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on the vendor's servers, allowing users to access the software through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and remote access, making it suitable for distributed teams. On the other hand, on-premise solutions are installed and run on the organization's servers.
