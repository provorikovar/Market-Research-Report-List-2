<p><p>Blu-ray media market encompasses different types including subscriptions, Video on Demand (VoD), and Electronic Sell-Through (EST). Subscriptions involve users paying a recurring fee to access a library of Blu-ray movies or TV shows, granting them unlimited viewing within the subscription period. VoD allows users to instantly rent or stream specific Blu-ray titles they desire, paying only for the content they consume. EST refers to users purchasing and downloading Blu-ray movies or shows digitally, enabling them to access and watch their content anytime, even without an internet connection.</p></p>
