<p><p>The blood transfusion filters market consists of three main types: whole blood transfusion, platelet transfusion, and red cell transfusion. Whole blood transfusions involve the transfer of all components of blood, including red cells, platelets, and plasma. Platelet transfusions, on the other hand, only involve the transfer of platelets to patients with low platelet counts. Red cell transfusions are specifically for patients with low red blood cell levels and aim to improve oxygen delivery to tissues. These different types of transfusions help address various medical conditions and improve patient health.</p></p>
