<p><p>Silicone Foley catheters are medical devices used to drain urine from the bladder. There are three types of Silicone Foley catheters in the market. The 2-way Silicone Foley catheter has two channels, one for urine drainage and one for inflation of a balloon to keep the catheter in place. The 3-way Silicone Foley catheter has an additional channel for irrigation with fluids. Similarly, the 4-way Silicone Foley catheter has an extra channel for continuous irrigation or instillation of medication. These variations cater to different patient needs and medical situations.</p></p>
