DTPMP (Diethylene Triamine Penta (Methylene Phosphonic Acid)) is commonly used as a scale and corrosion inhibitor. BHMTPMPA (Bis(HexaMethylene Triamine Penta(Methylene Phosphonic Acid))) is used in the textile and water treatment industries, while HPAA (2-Hydroxyphosphonocarboxylic Acid) is utilized as a water treatment agent.</p></p>
