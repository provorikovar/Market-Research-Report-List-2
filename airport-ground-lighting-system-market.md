<p><p>The Airport Ground Lighting System market can be categorized into various types. Approach Lights guide pilots during landing, illuminating the runway's approach path. Runway Lights provide visibility for safe takeoff and landing, ensuring optimal visibility and guidance. Taxiway and Apron Lights help aircraft maneuver on the ground while taxiing or parking. Stop Bars are used at intersections to prevent aircraft from crossing active runways. Lastly, the market also includes other lighting systems that aid in airport operations such as gate guidance lights or runway guard lights. These lighting systems collectively ensure safe and efficient aircraft movement on the ground.</p></p>
