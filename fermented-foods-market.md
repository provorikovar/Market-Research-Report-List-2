<p><p>Fermented foods are a type of processed food produced through the natural fermentation of raw ingredients by microorganisms, such as bacteria, yeast, or molds. The increasing consumer preference for natural and healthy food choices, along with a growing awareness of the health benefits of fermented foods, is driving the market growth. Fermented foods are known to improve gut health, boost the immune system, aid digestion, and provide essential nutrients and probiotics.</p><p>Another significant factor contributing to the market growth is the rise in vegan and vegetarian diets. Many plant-based alternatives, such as tempeh and plant-based yogurt, are produced through fermentation, attracting a broader consumer base.
