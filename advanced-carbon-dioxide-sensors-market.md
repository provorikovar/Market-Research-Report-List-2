<p><p>The Advanced Carbon Dioxide Sensors market consists of three main types of sensors: Infrared Carbon Dioxide Sensors, Catalytic Carbon Dioxide Sensors, and Heat Conduction Carbon Dioxide Sensors. Infrared Carbon Dioxide Sensors measure CO2 levels by detecting the absorption of infrared light. Catalytic Carbon Dioxide Sensors utilize a catalyst to facilitate the reaction between CO2 and a sensitive material. Heat Conduction Carbon Dioxide Sensors measure CO2 levels by sensing changes in thermal conductivity. These sensors have applications in various industries including HVAC systems, indoor air quality monitoring, and industrial emission control.</p></p>
