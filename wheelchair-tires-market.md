<p><p>The wheelchair tires market has three main types: air filled, solid, and foam filled. Air filled tires are similar to traditional bike tires and provide a comfortable and smooth ride, but they require regular maintenance. Solid tires are puncture-proof and low maintenance, making them ideal for indoor use, but they offer a less comfortable ride. Foam filled tires are a combination of air and solid tires, as they are puncture-proof and maintenance-free while still providing a comfortable ride.</p></p>
