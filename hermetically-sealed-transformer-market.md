<p><p>The hermetically sealed transformer market offers three types of transformers: single phase, dual phase, and three phase. </p><p>Single phase transformers are commonly used in residential and small-scale commercial applications. They have two primary terminals and are designed to convert single-phase alternating current (AC) to a lower voltage for use in household appliances and lighting.</p><p>Dual phase transformers have three primary terminals and provide two-phase AC power, which is typically used in motor-driven applications.</p><p>Three phase transformers have three primary terminals and are used in high-power industrial and commercial applications to convert three-phase AC power to a lower voltage for efficient transmission and distribution.</p></p>
