<p><strong>The PTFE-Lined Butterfly Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wafer Type</li><li>Double Flanged Type</li><li>Lug Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE-Lined Butterfly Valves are available in three main types: Wafer Type, Double Flanged Type, and Lug Type. </p><p>Wafer Type valves are designed to fit between two flanges and are held in place by bolts. They are lightweight, compact, and suitable for low-pressure applications. </p><p>Double Flanged Type valves have flanges on both ends, allowing them to be directly connected to pipes or equipment. They provide better sealing and are used in high-pressure applications. </p><p>Lug Type valves have threaded inserts on both sides, enabling them to be conveniently installed and removed from a pipeline. They are commonly used for medium-pressure applications.</p></p>
