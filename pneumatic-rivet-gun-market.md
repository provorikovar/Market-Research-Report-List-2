<p><p>The Pneumatic Rivet Gun market consists of different types, including Core Pulling Rivets, Rivet Nut Gun, and Ring Groove Rivet Gun. Core Pulling Rivets are used to fasten two materials together by pulling a solid core through a hollow rivet body. Rivet Nut Gun is designed to insert threaded rivet nuts into various materials, providing a strong and reliable connection. Ring Groove Rivet Gun is used for attachments that require a ring groove rivet head, providing a secure and durable joining solution. These various types cater to different riveting needs in several industries.</p></p>
