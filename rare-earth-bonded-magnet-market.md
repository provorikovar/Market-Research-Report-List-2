<p><p>The rare-earth bonded magnet market primarily consists of two types: NdFeB magnet market and SmCo magnet market. NdFeB magnets are made of neodymium, iron, and boron, offering high magnetic strength and excellent performance. They are widely used in various applications like motors, generators, and electronics. On the other hand, SmCo magnets are made of samarium and cobalt, providing high magnetic energy and resistance to corrosion. They are especially suitable for applications where stability in extreme conditions is required, such as aerospace and military electronics. Both types serve different industries due to their unique properties.</p></p>
