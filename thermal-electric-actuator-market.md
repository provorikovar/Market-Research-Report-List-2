<p><p>The Thermal Electric Actuator Market comprises two main types: Normally Closed Type and Normally Open Type. The Normally Closed Type actuator functions in a default closed position and requires an electric current to be applied to open it, ensuring a controlled operational state. On the other hand, the Normally Open Type actuator operates in a default open position and closes upon an electric current being introduced. Both types play crucial roles in diverse industries, aiding in the smooth functioning of valves, gates, and other mechanisms.</p></p>
