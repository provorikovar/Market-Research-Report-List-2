<p><ul><li>Direct Molecular DNA Probe</li><li>Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vaginal Panel Testing market includes two types of tests: Direct Molecular DNA Probe and Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT). Direct Molecular DNA Probe is a diagnostic test that directly detects specific DNA sequences in the vaginal sample. It identifies the presence of certain pathogens or genetic abnormalities. On the other hand, Molecular Nucleic Acid Amplification Test (NAAT) is a technique that amplifies the DNA or RNA in a vaginal sample to detect the presence of infectious agents, such as bacteria or viruses. It offers high sensitivity and specificity in diagnosing vaginal infections.</p></p>
