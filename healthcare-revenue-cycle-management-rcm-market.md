On-Premises RCM involves having the software and hardware installed locally at healthcare providers' premises, giving them more control and customization options. In contrast, Cloud-Based RCM is a web-based solution where the software and data are stored and accessed remotely over the internet. This type offers scalability, flexibility, and cost-effectiveness, as providers don't need to invest in infrastructure and can access the system from anywhere.</p></p>
