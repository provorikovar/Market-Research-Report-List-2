<p><p>The vehicle restraints market comprises three main types: hydraulic, electric, and mechanical. Hydraulic restraints use hydraulic pressure to secure vehicles in place, offering a strong and reliable holding force. Electric restraints, on the other hand, rely on electrical power to secure vehicles, providing ease of use and quick operation. Mechanical restraints, as the name suggests, employ mechanical components like hooks or bars to physically prevent vehicles from moving. Each type has its own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the vehicle restraint market.</p></p>
