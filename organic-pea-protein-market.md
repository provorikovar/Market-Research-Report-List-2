<p><p>The organic pea protein market consists of three main types: isolates, concentrates, and textured protein. Isolates are the purest form of pea protein and contain a higher protein content, with minimal carbohydrates and fats. Concentrates are less refined, retaining more of the natural pea protein and containing a slightly lower protein content. Textured proteins are processed to have a fibrous texture, making them suitable for use as meat substitutes. Each type offers different protein levels and properties, catering to various dietary needs and preferences.</p></p>
