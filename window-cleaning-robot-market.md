<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Window Cleaning Robot market consists of two main types: Vacuum Suction and Fan Adsorption. Vacuum Suction robots utilize a powerful vacuum to cling onto glass surfaces and move around, cleaning as they go. They offer strong suction power and are ideal for cleaning large, smooth glass surfaces. On the other hand, Fan Adsorption robots use the principle of air pressure to adhere to windows and clean them. These robots use built-in fans to create a vacuum seal, making them suitable for various types of windows and offering effective cleaning performance.</p></p>
