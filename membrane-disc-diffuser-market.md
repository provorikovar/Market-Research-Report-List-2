<p><p>A membrane disc diffuser is a device that is widely used in wastewater treatment plants to improve the aeration process. It consists of a flat disc made of durable materials such as EPDM or silicone, which is covered with small perforations. When air is pumped through the disc, it creates small bubbles, which rise to the water surface, increasing the oxygen transfer efficiency.</p><p>The current outlook of the membrane disc diffuser market is positive, and it is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years.
