<p><p>Heim joints, also known as rod ends or spherical bearings, come in different market types such as internal threads and external threads. Internal threads refer to heim joints that have the threading on the inside of the joint, allowing for connection with an externally threaded component. On the other hand, external threads refer to heim joints that have the threading on the outside of the joint, enabling connection with an internally threaded component. These market types provide versatility in designing connections for various applications.</p></p>
