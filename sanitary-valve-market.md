<p><p>The sanitary valve market encompasses various types of valves, including ball valves, butterfly valves, diaphragm valves, and others. Ball valves consist of a spherical closure that controls the flow via a hole in the center. Butterfly valves feature a disc rotating within the pipe to manage the flow. Diaphragm valves utilize a flexible membrane to regulate the flow passage. Additionally, there are other valve types available, each designed to meet specific sanitary requirements. These valves play a crucial role in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and water treatment to maintain hygienic and safe operations.</p></p>
