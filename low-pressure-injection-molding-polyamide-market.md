<p><p>Low pressure injection molding polyamide market types include granular polyamides, sheet polyamides, and powder polyamides. Granular polyamides are in the form of small grains, while sheet polyamides have a flat and thin structure. Powder polyamides are finely ground materials. These different market types cater to specific product requirements and preferences. Granular polyamides are commonly used for general molding applications, sheet polyamides are suitable for thin and flexible products, and powder polyamides are preferred for intricate and detailed designs.</p></p>
