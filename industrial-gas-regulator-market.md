<p><ul><li>Single-Stage</li><li>Dual-Stage</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Gas Regulator market can be divided into two types - single-stage and dual-stage regulators. Single-stage regulators are designed to reduce the pressure of the gas from the cylinder to a lower pressure in a single step. They are suitable for applications where constant pressure is not critical. On the other hand, dual-stage regulators have two chambers that allow for a more precise and stable pressure control. They are ideal for applications where a constant pressure is essential, such as in welding or laboratory work.</p></p>
