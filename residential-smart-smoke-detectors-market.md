<p><strong>The Residential Smart Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Dual Sensors</li><li>Ionization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The residential smart smoke detectors market consists of different types - photoelectric, dual sensors, and ionization detectors. Photoelectric detectors use light sensors to detect smoldering fires, while dual sensors combine both photoelectric and ionization technologies for a comprehensive detection of both smoldering and fast-flaming fires. Ionization detectors use a small amount of radioactive material to detect tiny smoke particles from fast-burning fires. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of consumers in terms of fire detection and safety in residential spaces.</p></p>
