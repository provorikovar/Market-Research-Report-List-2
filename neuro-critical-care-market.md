The market is driven by factors such as technological advancements in neurocritical care devices, a growing geriatric population, increasing investments in the healthcare sector, and rising awareness about neurocritical care among patients and healthcare providers. Additionally, the development of innovative drugs and therapies for neurological disorders further propels market growth.</p><p>Moreover, the COVID-19 pandemic has also led to a surge in demand for neuro critical care services, as the virus can cause severe neurological complications in some patients.
