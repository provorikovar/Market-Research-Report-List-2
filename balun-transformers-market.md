<p><ul><li>Multilayer Balun Transformers</li><li>Single-layer Balun Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Balun Transformers are used in electronic systems to convert between balanced and unbalanced signals. There are two main types, Multilayer Balun Transformers and Single-layer Balun Transformers. Multilayer Balun Transformers have multiple windings on separate layers, allowing for higher performance and better control of impedance. They are suitable for high-frequency applications. On the other hand, Single-layer Balun Transformers have all windings on a single layer, making them more cost-effective but with limited performance. These are commonly used in low-frequency applications.</p></p>
