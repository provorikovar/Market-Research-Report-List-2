On the other hand, Type II market involves the production of bio-based adipic acids through renewable resources such as biomass, plant-derived sugars, and other sustainable feedstocks. This type of bio-based adipic acid production is considered more environmentally friendly and aligned with the principles of sustainability.</p></p>
