<p><strong>The Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS)</li><li>Circuit Switched Fall Back (CFSB)</li><li>Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE)</li><li>Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA)</li><li>Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voice Over Long-Term Evolution (VoLTE) is a technology that enables voice calls over LTE networks, providing high-quality voice services. It has different market types such as Voice Over IP Multimedia Subsystem (VoIMS), which uses IMS platforms to deliver VoLTE services. Circuit Switched Fall Back (CSFB) allows LTE devices to fall back to 2G/3G networks for voice calls. Dual Radio/Simultaneous Voice and LTE (SVLTE) uses two radios simultaneously for voice and data services. Voice Over LTE Via Generic Access Network (VOLGA) integrates VoLTE with Wi-Fi networks. Single Radio Voice Call Continuity (SRVCC) allows seamless handover between LTE and 2G/3G networks for voice calls.</p></p>
