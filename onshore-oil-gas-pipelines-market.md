<p><ul><li>Gathering Pipelines</li><li>Transportation Pipelines</li><li>Distribution Pipelines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Onshore oil and gas pipelines can be categorized into three main types: gathering pipelines, transportation pipelines, and distribution pipelines. Gathering pipelines are used to collect crude oil and natural gas from multiple wellheads and transport them to processing facilities. Transportation pipelines are responsible for long-distance transportation of oil and gas from production sites to refineries or distribution centers. Finally, distribution pipelines deliver the refined products to end-users such as homes, businesses, and industrial facilities. These pipelines form a crucial infrastructure for the efficient and safe movement of oil and gas across onshore regions.</p></p>
