<p><p>A handheld corona surface treater is a portable device that is used to treat the surfaces of various materials with corona discharge. Corona discharge is an electrical discharge that occurs when a high voltage is applied to a conductor in close proximity to a dielectric material. This process alters the surface properties of the material, making it more receptive to adhesion, coating, or printing.</p><p>The handheld corona surface treater market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The primary factors driving this growth are the increasing demand for surface treatment in various industries such as packaging, automotive, electronics, and medical devices.
