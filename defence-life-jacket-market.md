<p><p>The Defence Life Jacket market offers three types of jackets: foam jackets, inflatable jackets, and hybrid jackets. Foam jackets are made from buoyant materials like foam and provide a constant level of buoyancy, ensuring the wearer stays afloat. Inflatable jackets, on the other hand, use gas cartridges or manual inflation to provide buoyancy when necessary. Hybrid jackets combine the benefits of foam and inflatable jackets, using foam for initial buoyancy and inflatable chambers for additional support. Each type caters to different preferences and requirements in the Defence Life Jacket market.</p></p>
