<p><strong>The Automatic Guided System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Electronic</li><li>Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Guided System (AGS) market encompasses three main types: Magnetic, Electronic, and Laser. Magnetic AGS relies on magnetic tapes or markers laid out on the floor to guide the system's path. It is a cost-effective and flexible solution suitable for various industries. Electronic AGS uses sensors to follow wires or signals embedded in the floor. This type offers precision and adaptability but can be more expensive. Laser AGS employs laser beams to detect and navigate obstacles accurately. It is a sophisticated and high-precision solution commonly used in complex industrial environments.</p></p>
